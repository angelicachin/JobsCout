ID/X Partners
Data Engineer

ID/X Partners was established in 2002 by ex-bankers and management consultants who have vast experiences in credit cycle and process management, scoring development, and performance management. Our combined experience has served corporations across Asia and Australia regions and in multiple industries, specifically financial services, telecommunications, manufacturing and retail.

ID/X Partners provides consulting services that specializes in utilizing data analytic and decisioning (DAD) solutions combined with an integrated risk management and marketing discipline to help clients optimize the portfolio profitability and business process.

Comprehensive consulting service and technology solutions offered by id/x partners makes it as a one-stop service provider. Our corporate core value is CHAMPION. CHAMPION are Customer first, Honorable, Agile, Mentorship, Proactive, Innovative, Ownership, and Numeric.

Vision

Creating new opportunities for businesses to better understand their customers, their industry and their own operations.


Mission
  • Deliver right solution and superior service for the customer to realize maximum business value and cultivate a creative working environment to solve tough problems
  • Focus on profitable growth without sacrificing integrity & quality and respond quickly and meaningfully to new opportunities (or threats)
  • Develop team’s continuous personal and professional growth and put forth new ideas in order to improve our work
  • Exercise accountability and ownership of our work and responsibilities and make decisions based on facts and numbers
Benefit
  • Data engineers typically work closely with data scientists, analysts, and other teams. This collaborative environment allows for exposure to different perspectives and skills, fostering a well-rounded professional experience.
  • Provide job security and potentially open doors to other career opportunities in the future.
  • Expand skill set by working with new technologies, tools, and methodologies.
Requirements
  • Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Information Technology, or a related field.
  • Minimum of 2.5+ years of professional experience in data engineering or a related role.
  • Strong proficiency in programming languages such as Python, Java, or Scala.
  • Extensive experience with data processing frameworks and tools, such as Apache Spark, Apache Flink, or Hadoop.
  • In-depth knowledge of data modeling, ETL (Extract, Transform, Load) processes, and data warehouse architecture.
  • Hands-on experience with cloud platforms, such as AWS, Azure, or Google Cloud.
  • Familiarity with database systems, both relational (e.g., PostgreSQL, MySQL) and NoSQL (e.g., MongoDB, Cassandra).
  • Expertise in building and optimizing data pipelines for large-scale data sets.
  • Strong understanding of data security and compliance standards.
  • Ability to collaborate effectively with cross-functional teams, including data scientists and business analysts.
  • Proven track record of designing and implementing scalable, reliable, and efficient data solutions.
  • Excellent problem-solving skills and the ability to adapt to evolving data engineering technologies.
