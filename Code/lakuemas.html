Laku Emas
Product Designer

Laku Emas is the first gold buying and selling service in Indonesia which can be accessed from the website and mobile application and is supported by branches in large malls in Indonesia, where customers can buy, store, sell and withdraw gold without any storage fees and get additional profits. We support the growth of the creative economy in the gold and jewelry industry by developing potential SMEs and helping manage customers' gold savings so they can provide competitive results.

Laku Emas provides convenience for consumers where consumers can buy, sell, send and withdraw gold without any storage fees and get additional benefits in the form of ease in withdrawing physical gold in almost 100 jewelry shops in almost all big cities in Indonesia. Apart from that, consumers can also exchange gold for jewelry at these shops and get various attractive promotions. Laku Emas sell various kinds of grams, ranging from 0.5 grams, 1 gram, 2 grams, 3 grams, 5 grams to 10 grams. PT Laku Emas Indonesia is a technology company that provides a digital platform where you can buy gold starting from IDR 50,000,- anytime and anywhere. All transactions are conducted conveniently in the palm of your hand and securely.
